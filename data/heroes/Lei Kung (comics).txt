'''Lei Kung the Thunderer''' is a fictional character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]]. His notable pupils include [[Iron Fist (comics)|Iron Fist]] and his son [[Steel Serpent]].
 
==Fictional character biography==
[[Yu-Ti]] apprenticed the young [[Iron Fist (comics)|Daniel Rand]] to Lei Kung the Thunderer, who teaches him the martial arts while in the mystical city of [[K'un-L'un]]. The [[Steel Serpent]] (the exiled son of Lei Kung) later becomes one of the adversaries of the Iron Fist as he coveted the Iron Fist power.<ref>''Marvel Premiere'' #15. Marvel Comics.</ref>

After she was busted for training three cavemen, [[Iron Fist (comics)#Fan Fei|Fan Fei]] was chained up and watched as the cavemen were fed to Shou-Lou by Lei Kung. When Fan Fei defeated Shou-Lao, Lei Kung had Fan Fei exiled from K'un-Lun. Sometime later, Lei Kung was at the entrance of K'un-Lun when Fan Fei came across it. Lei Kung states that her sentencing was wrong and wanted to bring her home. Fan Fei declined stating that Earth was her home and her fights here are just the beginning.<ref>''Avengers'' Vol. 8 #13. Marvel Comics.</ref>

When Steel Serpent failed to defeat [[Shou-Lao]], Lei Kung found him weeping in the snow. He then took Steel Serpent back to the city.<ref>''Marvel Team-Up'' #64. Marvel Comics.</ref>

During the ''[[Avengers vs. X-Men]]'' storyline, Iron Fist brings Lei Kung to take [[Hope Summers (comics)|Hope]] to K'un-L'un for training.<ref>{{cite comic |writer=Fraction, Matt |penciller=Coipel, Oliver |inker=Morales, Mark |colorist=Martin, Laura |title=Avengers vs. X-Men |issue=7 |date=July 2012 |publisher=Marvel Comics |id=}}</ref> As the [[Phoenix Force (comics)|Phoenix Force]]-powered [[Namor]] attacked [[Wakanda]], [[Iron Man]] told Lei Kung to pull Hope Summers back to K'un-L'un and seal the portal before Namor gets through. The [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]] later arrived in K'un-L'un.<ref>''Avengers vs. X-Men'' #8. Marvel Comics.</ref> When the Phoenix Force-powered [[Cyclops (Marvel Comics)|Cyclops]] attacked K'un-L'un, Lei Kung met with Hope Summers outside the Cave of Shou-Lao telling her that it's time for her final lesson. They rode Shou-Lao into battle where they were blasted out of the air by the Phoenix Force-powered Cyclops. Tapping into Shou-Lao's powers, Hope Summers used a special punch to send Cyclops back to Earth where the punch also temporarily incapacitated him.<ref>''Avengers vs. X-Men'' #10. Marvel Comics.</ref> As K'un-L'un was being rebuilt following the resulting battle, Lei Kung held a small boy on his shoulders as he tells Iron Man that they don't need help in the reconstruction of the city. He did counsel Iron Man telling him that magic and science are not as divided as he thinks. Iron Man then leaves at Lei Kung's suggestion to handle the damage in his area.<ref>''AVX: Consequences'' #3. Marvel Comics.</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
The weapons master of K'un-Lun, Lei Kung is a martial arts expert, one of the finest in the Marvel universe.

==In other media==
Lei Kung appears in the Netflix web series ''[[Iron Fist (TV series)|Iron Fist]]'', portrayed by [[Hoon Lee]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ign.com/articles/2017/03/17/marvels-iron-fist-episode-6-immortal-emerges-from-cave-review|title=Marvel's Iron Fist Episode 6: "Immortal Emerges from Cave" Review|publisher=[[IGN]]|last=Vejvoda|first=Jim|date=March 17, 2017|accessdate=March 21, 2017|deadurl=no}}</ref> The parts with him being Steel Serpent's father and Daniel Rand's mentor are intact in this series. Lei Kung first appears in the episode "Immortal Emerges from Cave" where he appears to Danny Rand in a vision during meditation recapping a K'un-L'un story. As Danny Rand does his fights with Andrei and Grigori Veznikov, [[Bride of Nine Spiders]], and [[Scythe (comics)|Scythe]], Lei Kung dispenses some wisdom which Danny uses in his fights against them. When Lei Kung's vision tells him to finish off Scythe, Madame Gao appeared to threaten to use her knife on Radovan Bernivig's captive daughter Sabina causing Danny Rand to cease finishing off Scythe. Though Lei Kung in Danny Rand's vision was disappointed in this, Danny Rand told him on the way out that Madame Gao cheated and that he had no other choice. Lei Kung's vision remain silent as Danny left with Sabina.<ref>{{cite episode|title=Immortal Emerges from Cave|series=Marvel's Iron Fist|credits=RZA (director); Dwain Worrell (writer)|network=[[Netflix]]|airdate=March 17, 2017|season=1|number=6}}</ref> In the episode "Black Tiger Steel Hearts," Davos tells Danny Rand that he was sent by Lei Kung to retrieve Danny while also mentioning that his father is concerned and angry with him.<ref>{{cite episode|title=Black Tiger Steals Heart|series=Marvel's Iron Fist|credits=Hoar, Peter (director); Quinton Peeples (writer)|network=[[Netflix]]|airdate=March 17, 2017|season=1|number=10}}</ref> In the episode "Lead Horse Back to Stable," it was mentioned in a humorous discussion between Danny and Davos that they once rode a chariot around the lake to see any bathing women from the village only to stumble upon Lei Kung meditating in the nude.<ref>{{cite episode|title=Lead Horse Back to Stable|series=Marvel's Iron Fist|credits=Chow, Deborah (director); Ian Stokes (writer)|network=[[Netflix]]|airdate=March 17, 2017|season=1|number=11}}</ref> He briefly reappears in the [[Iron Fist (season 2)|second season]] where in flashback, he and his fellow Order of the Crane Mother members [[Yu-Ti|YÃ¼-Ti]] and Priya watch Danny Rand and [[Steel Serpent|Davos]] fight for the right to face Shou-Lao. When Danny turns the tide against Davos and tries to get him to yield, Lei Kung ends the fight declaring that Danny will face Shou-Lao.<ref>{{cite episode|title=The City's Not for Burning|series=Marvel's Iron Fist|credits=Talalay, Rachel (director); Jon Worley (writer)|network=[[Netflix]]|airdate=September 7, 2018|season=2|number=2}}</ref>
